隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 22 日

Strivory 是一款用于查看、整理和导出个人运动年历的 iPhone 应用。我们将隐私设计为产品的默认状态：Strivory 不要求创建账户，也不运营开发者服务器来收集你的运动记录。

我们如何处理数据

Apple 健康

在你授权后，Strivory 仅读取 Apple 健康中的 Workout（训练）记录，用于计算活动日、运动分类和运动年历。Strivory 不会向 Apple 健康写入数据。除非你主动开启下方所述的 iCloud 备份，Workout 不会离开你的设备。

CSV 导入

你选择导入的 CSV 文件会在设备本地解析。导入后的运动记录保存在你的设备上，用于生成年历；你可以在应用中删除导入批次，或通过删除应用移除其本地数据。

iCloud 备份与恢复

此功能默认关闭。你主动开启后，Strivory 会将已读取的 Workout 快照、CSV 导入批次、导入策略和显示名称备份到由同一 Apple ID 控制的 Strivory 私有 CloudKit 数据库。备份用于在重新安装应用或使用另一台设备时恢复你的运动年历。该数据库不是公开数据库，不会与其他 Strivory 用户共享，也不会上传至开发者自有服务器。

你可随时在 Strivory“设置”中关闭备份。关闭后会停止新的同步，但不会自动删除既有 iCloud 备份；如需移除备份，请先在应用中删除相应 CSV 批次并同步，或在 Apple 的 iCloud 存储管理中管理 Strivory 数据。删除应用只会移除本机数据，不会删除该 iCloud 备份。

导出与分享

当你主动选择“保存到相册”时，Strivory 会请求仅添加照片的权限，并将生成的 PNG 图片写入你的照片图库。当你主动选择分享时，内容将由 iOS 系统分享面板发送到你选择的目的地；该目的地的隐私实践由其自身政策决定。

我们不收集的数据

Strivory 不创建用户账户，不使用广告 SDK，不使用第三方分析或追踪 SDK，也不会收集、出售、出租或共享你的 Workout、CSV 记录、照片或设备标识符。若你开启 iCloud 备份，相关运动快照与导入记录会传输到 Apple 提供的、仅属于你的私有 iCloud 空间，以提供备份和恢复功能。

你的控制权

  • 你可以随时在 iOS 的“健康”或“设置”中撤销 Strivory 的健康数据读取权限。
  • 你可以在应用中删除 CSV 导入批次。
  • 删除应用会移除由 Strivory 保存在该设备上的本地应用数据；已启用的 iCloud 备份可在重新安装后恢复。

儿童隐私

Strivory 不面向儿童收集个人信息，也不要求创建账户。

政策更新

若本政策发生实质变化，我们会在本页更新生效日期。继续使用 Strivory 即表示你接受更新后的政策。

联系我们

如有隐私问题，请发送邮件至 strivoryapp@gmail.com，或通过 支持页面 联系我们。

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Strivory is an iPhone app for viewing, organizing, and exporting a personal movement calendar. Privacy is the default: Strivory does not require an account and does not operate developer servers that collect your workout history.

How data is handled

Apple Health

With your permission, Strivory reads Workout records from Apple Health only to calculate active days, workout categories, and movement calendars. Strivory never writes data to Apple Health. Workout data stays on your device unless you choose to enable the iCloud Backup described below.

CSV imports

CSV files you choose are parsed locally on your device. Imported records are kept on your device for calendar generation. You can delete an import batch in the app or remove the app to remove its local data.

iCloud Backup & Restore

This feature is off by default. When you choose to enable it, Strivory backs up Workout snapshots it has read, CSV import batches, import strategies, and your display name to a private Strivory CloudKit database controlled by the same Apple ID. The backup helps restore your movement calendar after reinstalling the app or using another device. It is not a public database, is never shared with other Strivory users, and is not uploaded to developer-owned servers.

You can turn backup off at any time in Strivory Settings. Turning it off stops future syncs but does not automatically delete an existing iCloud backup. To remove backup data, delete the relevant CSV batches in the app and sync, or manage Strivory data in Apple's iCloud storage settings. Deleting the app removes only local app data, not an enabled iCloud backup.

Exporting and sharing

When you choose Save to Photos, Strivory requests add-only photo access and writes the generated PNG to your library. When you choose Share, iOS sends content to the destination you select; that destination is governed by its own privacy policy.

Data we do not collect

Strivory has no user accounts, advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your Workout records, CSV records, photos, or device identifiers. If you enable iCloud Backup, the relevant workout snapshots and import records are transmitted to Apple's private iCloud storage for your backup and restore functionality.

Your choices

  • You can revoke Health data access in iOS Health or Settings at any time.
  • You can delete CSV import batches in the app.
  • Deleting the app removes Strivory's local app data from that device; an enabled iCloud backup can be restored after reinstalling.
