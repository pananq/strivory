支持

Strivory 帮你把 Apple 健康和 CSV 记录整理成可导出的运动年历。

开始使用

  1. 打开 Strivory 后，允许读取 Apple 健康中的 Workout 记录。
  2. 应用会在启动和回到前台时自动刷新；也可使用左上角按钮手动同步。
  3. 可通过 CSV 导入补录日期与运动类型。
  4. 选择年份和海报模板，生成 PNG 后保存到照片或分享。

常见问题

为什么没有显示 Workout？

请确认 iPhone 上已有 Apple 健康 Workout 记录，并在“健康”或“设置”中允许 Strivory 读取 Workout。少于 10 分钟的训练会被自动过滤。

如何调整应用语言？

在 Strivory 的“设置”中选择简体中文或 English。该设置也会影响新导出的图片。

如何删除 CSV 数据？

在主页底部的“CSV 导入批次”中删除对应批次即可。

保存图片失败怎么办？

请在 iOS“设置”中允许 Strivory 添加照片，然后重新生成并保存图片。

联系支持

如需帮助，请发送邮件至 strivoryapp@gmail.com。为便于处理，请附上你的 iPhone 型号、iOS 版本和问题截图（如适用）。

Support

Strivory turns Apple Health and CSV records into an exportable movement calendar.

Getting started

  1. Allow Strivory to read Workout records from Apple Health.
  2. The app refreshes when it launches and returns to the foreground; use the top-left button to refresh manually.
  3. Use a CSV file to add historical dates and workout types.
  4. Select years and a poster template, then generate a PNG to save or share.

Frequently asked questions

Why are no workouts shown?

Make sure your iPhone has Apple Health Workout records and that Strivory can read Workouts in Health or Settings. Workouts shorter than 10 minutes are filtered out.

How do I change the app language?

Choose Simplified Chinese or English in Strivory Settings. The selection also applies to newly exported images.

How do I delete CSV data?

Delete the relevant batch under CSV Import Batches on the Home screen.

How do I contact support?

For help, email strivoryapp@gmail.com. Please include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a screenshot when relevant.
